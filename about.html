About Pla Title LLC

Georgia-rooted. Nationwide reach. Built on integrity, accuracy, and service.

Our Story

Pla Title LLC was founded with a straightforward mission: eliminate the frustration businesses and individuals face when navigating complex vehicle title and registration requirements. What started as a Georgia-based tag agency quickly grew into a full-service, nationwide operation, serving dealerships, fleet operators, financial institutions, and individual clients across all 50 states.

We built our reputation by doing the hard work — mastering every state's DMV rules, building direct relationships with county offices, and delivering results that meet deadlines. Today, Pla Title LLC is the trusted partner companies call when accuracy and speed are non-negotiable.

Our Mission & Values

Accuracy First

A single error on a title document can cost weeks and thousands of dollars. We treat every file with the precision it demands, reviewing each submission before it leaves our hands.

Transparent Communication

No surprises. We keep clients informed at every step — from intake to completion — so you always know exactly where your paperwork stands.

Leadership

Plato E. Autridge Jr.

Chief Executive Officer & Founder

Plato E. Autridge Jr. founded Pla Title LLC with a vision to modernize the vehicle title and registration industry. With deep roots in Georgia's automotive and regulatory landscape, Plato recognized a critical gap: businesses were losing time and money navigating fragmented, state-by-state DMV processes with no reliable partner to guide them.

Under his leadership, Pla Title LLC has grown from a local Georgia tag agency into a nationally recognized title services company. Plato brings hands-on expertise in title law, fleet management, and regulatory compliance, personally ensuring that every client relationship is grounded in trust, transparency, and results. His commitment to accuracy and client service sets the standard for everything the company does.
