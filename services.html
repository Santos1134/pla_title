Our Services

For all your tag & title needs — across all 159 Georgia counties.

For All Your Tag & Title Needs

Pla Title LLC processes new and pre-owned vehicle titles, state transfers, and registration renewals at an enterprise scale — across all 159 Georgia counties. Whether you manage a fleet of 10 or 10,000 vehicles, our team handles every category of tag and title work with accuracy and speed.

Vehicle Title & Registration

We take the complexity out of getting your vehicle properly titled and registered. New purchases, used vehicles, or out-of-state conversions — we get it done right the first time.

State Transfer

Moved to Georgia or heading out of state? We coordinate the title and registration switch on your behalf so you stay compliant without the back-and-forth with multiple agencies.

Salvage, Repossession & Bonded Titles

When a vehicle's ownership history is complicated — whether it's a repo, a salvage situation, or a missing title — we know exactly how to cut through the red tape and get it resolved.

Title & Registration Correction

Errors on your title or registration can cause real headaches down the road. We catch and correct documentation mistakes quickly so your records stay clean and accurate.

Registration Renewals

Stop worrying about expiration dates. We handle renewals for single vehicles or large fleets — keeping everything current so you never deal with late fees or compliance gaps.

IRP Titles

Commercial carriers crossing state lines need apportioned plates and multi-state registrations. We manage the International Registration Plan process from start to finish.

Duplicate Titles

Lost or damaged your title? We'll get a replacement issued through the Georgia DOR as quickly as possible — no need to navigate the state system on your own.

Change Lien Holder

Refinanced your vehicle or paid off your loan? We update the lender information on your title so your records accurately reflect who holds — or no longer holds — a financial interest.

Replacement Decals, Stickers & Plates

If your registration sticker, decal, or plate is lost, stolen, or damaged, we'll get you replacements without the wait. Stay street-legal without the trip to the county office.

Lien / Security Interest Filing

We handle the proper recording of security interests on vehicle titles for lenders and financial institutions — keeping your collateral documentation airtight and fully compliant.

Specialty & Prestige Tags

Want something beyond the standard plate? From personalized vanity tags to organization and military plates, we help you order and obtain specialty tags throughout Georgia.

Title History

Need to know the full story behind a vehicle? We pull complete ownership and title records — useful for pre-purchase checks, legal matters, or internal fleet audits.

Lessee Updates

Lease terms change — and so do lessees. We keep the title and registration records current whenever there's a change in who's driving or who's responsible for a leased vehicle.

Address Update

Moved recently? We update your address across all relevant DMV records so renewal notices reach you and your registration stays valid without any interruption.

Ownership Transfer

Buying or selling a vehicle should be straightforward. We take care of the title assignment, odometer statement, and every required form so the transaction closes without a hitch.

IRP Service Provider

We act as your dedicated IRP account manager — handling multi-jurisdiction apportioned registrations, annual renewals, and fleet additions for commercial operators across the country.
